# Conference Guidelines: ICLR 2026

## Venue
ICLR 2026 — International Conference on Learning Representations

## Submission Format
- Double-blind review
- PDF submission via OpenReview

## Page Limits
- Main body: 9 pages (including all content up to and including the Conclusion section)
- References: unlimited pages, not counted toward page limit
- Appendix: unlimited pages, not counted toward page limit

## Formatting
- Use the official ICLR LaTeX template
- Font: Times New Roman or similar serif font
- Single-column format
- natbib citation style (\citep, \citet)

## Required Sections
- Abstract (max 250 words)
- Introduction
- Related Work (or Background)
- Method
- Experiments
- Conclusion
- References

## Supplementary Material
- Appendix allowed for proofs, additional experiments, hyperparameters
- Code submission encouraged but not required

## Review Criteria
1. Quality: Technical soundness and rigor
2. Clarity: Writing quality and presentation
3. Originality: Novelty of contribution
4. Significance: Importance of the contribution

## Submission Deadline
Typically October 2025 for ICLR 2026 (research cutoff date: September 2025)

## Citation Cutoff Date
For literature review purposes: September 2025 (papers published after this date may not be known to reviewers)
